About me

    I am an MBA Candidate at the MIT Sloan School of Management. Prior to business school, I worked as an economic consultant at Analysis Group in Boston. In this role, I applied financial and economic analysis to complex business litigation. While at Analysis Group, my primary area of expertise included evaluating damages in antitrust and intellectual property cases, particularly in the technology sector.

    At MIT Sloan, I am interested in exploring operations management and supply chain logistics in the technology industry.

    Outside of school, I am avid reader of the Econoblogsophere and enjoy cooking. I am also an actively involved in the LGBTQ community in the greater Boston area.
